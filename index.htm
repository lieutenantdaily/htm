About Me!

Hey! My name is Morgan.

I am an aunt to my two favorite little girls. I love traveling, dessert, dinosaur things, & Anthropologie (but with an eBay price tag).

I love photography and telling stories. I want our sessions to capture you as you are right now, without erasing any detail. My style is very semi-lifestyle; sometimes posed, but without feeling overly posed. I also have a heart for kids and my favorite subject is an energetic 5 year old.

Laughter is my top requirement! You can’t be afraid to be you in all your glory, beautiful mess that you are.

I would be honored to share a part of your story with you and your loved ones. Let’s get coffee and figure out the rest!
